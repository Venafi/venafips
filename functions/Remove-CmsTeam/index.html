    Remove-CmsTeam¶

    SYNOPSIS¶

    Remove a team

    SYNTAX¶

    Remove-CmsTeam [-ID] <String> [[-ThrottleLimit] <Int32>] [[-TrustClient] <TrustClient>]
 [-ProgressAction <ActionPreference>] [-WhatIf] [-Confirm] [<CommonParameters>]

    DESCRIPTION¶

    Remove a team from Certificate Manager, SaaS

    EXAMPLES¶

    EXAMPLE 1¶

    Remove-CmsTeam -ID 'ca7ff555-88d2-4bfc-9efa-2630ac44c1f2'
Remove a team

    EXAMPLE 2¶

    Remove-CmsTeam -ID 'ca7ff555-88d2-4bfc-9efa-2630ac44c1f2' -Confirm:$false
Remove a team bypassing the confirmation prompt
